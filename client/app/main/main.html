ABOUT US

Canteen + Co. makes eating better possible. We’re three former food truckers with an obsession with food - and a passion for people. We looked for clean food on the streets of Boston… but no such luck. We decided to bring clean food to you, and we’re inviting you to join us. We got our start hosting a recurring community dinners as The Community Canteen. Once a month, we served up simple seasonal bowls to a rotating group of members. Turns out that people liked eating our food - and loved eating together. We provide good clean food for gatherings. With our seasonal bowls and consciously sourced snacks we’re launching a new way of eating. Get connected with our community to join us for a dinner, follow us to find out where popping up next, or give us a ring to ask about our catering services. Let’s eat better, together.
